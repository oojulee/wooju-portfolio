GovDeals Redesign

Redesigned the GovDeals website to address outdated design, inconsistent typography, and complex navigation, transforming it into a modern, user-centered platform by improving accessibility, streamlining navigation, and implementing core features such as authentication, item listings, and buying/selling functionality.

Our Mission

Challenges & Solutions

Pain points identified

  • Cluttered, visually inconsistent UI with no clear hierarchy
  • Navigation required too many steps to find relevant items
  • No responsive design — broken layouts on mobile devices
  • Outdated typography and low color contrast hurting accessibility
  • Fragmented authentication and user account flows

Design decisions made

  • Established a clean design system with consistent tokens in Figma
  • Redesigned information architecture to reduce navigation depth
  • Built responsive layouts with mobile-first approach
  • Improved color contrast ratios and accessible type scale
  • Unified auth flow — sign in, register, and session management
